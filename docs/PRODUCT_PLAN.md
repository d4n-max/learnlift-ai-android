# LearnLift AI Product Plan

## Product Summary

LearnLift AI is an Android skill-building and study coach app that helps users build job-ready and exam-ready skills through daily study sessions, flashcards, quizzes, progress tracking, and future AI-assisted explanations.

## App Identity

- App name: LearnLift AI
- Tagline: Elevate Your Skills, Effortlessly.
- Platform: Android
- Package name: com.learnliftai.app

## Product Positioning

LearnLift AI is a general skill-building and study coach app, not limited to one niche. The app should support multiple study paths from the beginning while keeping the first version simple and focused.

## Initial Study Paths

1. English Vocabulary & Speaking Prep
2. Job Interview Prep
3. IT / QA Interview Prep as an optional sample path

## Future Study Paths

- Driving License Exam Prep
- School Exam Prep
- Certification Prep
- Software Testing Prep
- Language Learning
- Career Skills

## Target Users

- Students
- Job seekers
- Career switchers
- English learners
- People preparing for interviews, exams, or certifications

## User Goals

- Build confidence through short, repeatable study sessions.
- Choose a study path that matches an immediate goal.
- Practice with flashcards and quizzes.
- Track progress and maintain a daily study habit.
- Eventually receive AI-assisted explanations for difficult topics.

## Product Goals

- Make studying feel focused, achievable, and motivating.
- Support multiple study paths without making the MVP complex.
- Organize local content so new study paths can be added later.
- Establish a clean Android foundation using Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Material 3, and simple MVVM.

## Core Experience

Users open the app, choose or continue a study path, see their progress, complete a daily study session, review flashcards, take quizzes, and build a streak through consistent practice.

## Future Expansion Ideas

- AI-assisted explanations.
- Personalized weak-area review.
- Content packs for additional exams, interviews, and certifications.
- Remote content updates.
- Optional accounts and cloud sync if needed later.
