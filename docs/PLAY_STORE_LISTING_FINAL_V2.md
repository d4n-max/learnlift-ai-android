## Full Description

LearnLift AI is a focused AI study coach for learners who want short, practical study sessions for interview preparation, English vocabulary, and IT / QA interview prep.

Choose a study path, build a daily learning habit, review flashcards, take quizzes, and track your progress locally on your device. LearnLift AI combines practical study tools with optional AI-powered study support, so you can understand mistakes, focus on weak topics, and keep improving with a clear next step.

What you can do with LearnLift AI:

- Practice English vocabulary and speaking confidence
- Prepare for job interview questions and answer structure
- Review IT / QA interview prep basics
- Study with flashcards and Smart Review spaced repetition
- Take quizzes with explanations
- Try Adaptive Quiz Mode to focus on weaker topics
- Complete short daily study sessions
- Track progress, streaks, scores, and topic weakness
- Use Smart Coach recommendations based on local progress
- Ask AI Coach for answer explanations when available
- Set local daily study reminders

Study paths included:

- English Vocabulary & Speaking Prep
- Job Interview Prep
- IT / QA Interview Prep

Free features include:

- Current study paths
- Flashcards
- Smart Review
- Quizzes
- Adaptive Quiz
- Daily Session
- Basic progress tracking
- Topic weakness tracking
- Rule-based Smart Coach tips
- Limited AI Coach previews
- Local daily reminders

Premium adds or expands:

- More AI Coach help each day
- AI-powered answer explanations
- AI Study Review
- 7-day AI Study Plan access
- Premium status and subscription support

Some advanced Premium features, such as deeper progress insights and future study packs, may be marked as coming soon until available.

LearnLift AI is designed for:

- Job seekers preparing for interviews
- English learners building work-ready vocabulary
- Beginner IT / QA learners reviewing core concepts
- Students and self-learners who prefer short, repeatable practice
- Anyone building a daily study habit

Privacy and data notes:

- No account is required
- No ads
- No cloud sync in the current version
- Normal progress is stored locally on your device
- Local reminders are scheduled on your device
- AI Coach requests are optional and user-initiated
- AI requests may send limited study or quiz context to the LearnLift AI backend only when you tap an AI action
- Premium purchases are handled by Google Play and RevenueCat

LearnLift AI does not guarantee job offers, interview results, exam results, certifications, or career outcomes. It is a study and practice tool designed to help you prepare with more focus and consistency.
