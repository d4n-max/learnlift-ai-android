## Full Description Draft

LearnLift AI is a focused study coach app for building job-ready and exam-ready skills through short daily practice.

Choose a study path, review practical flashcards, answer quiz questions with explanations, complete daily study sessions, and track your progress locally on your device. The first MVP includes study content for English practice, job interview preparation, and beginner IT / QA interview preparation.

LearnLift AI is designed to feel clean, professional, and motivating. It helps learners build confidence through simple, repeatable study habits without requiring an account or cloud sync.

This internal testing build is an early MVP. Live AI Coach features, accounts, cloud sync, payments, and certificates are not included yet.
