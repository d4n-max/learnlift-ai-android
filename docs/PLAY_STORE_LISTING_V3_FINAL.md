## Full Description

LearnLift AI Study Coach helps you build a practical daily learning habit with flashcards, quizzes, adaptive practice, progress tracking, and optional AI-powered study support.

Use LearnLift AI to review interview prep, English vocabulary, QA concepts, SQL basics, and technical learning topics in short, focused sessions. Choose a study path, practice with flashcards and quizzes, review weak topics, and use Smart Coach guidance to decide what to study next.

LearnLift AI is designed as a study and practice tool. It does not guarantee job offers, interview results, exam results, language fluency, certifications, or career outcomes.

## What You Can Do

- Study with flashcards and Smart Review spaced repetition
- Take quizzes with clear answer feedback
- Use Adaptive Quiz to focus on weaker topics
- Track progress, streaks, quiz scores, and weak topics
- Complete short daily study sessions
- Get local Smart Coach recommendations
- Ask AI Coach for answer explanations when available
- Generate AI Quiz Review when available
- Build a 7-day AI Study Plan with Premium when available
- Set optional local study reminders
- Preview available Premium Study Packs before upgrading
