# LearnLift AI Brand Guide

## App Name

LearnLift AI

## Tagline

Elevate Your Skills, Effortlessly.

## Brand Colors

- Primary purple: `#553C9A`
- Accent pink: `#ED64A6`

## Color Usage

### Primary Purple

Use `#553C9A` as the main brand color. Suggested uses:

- Top app bars
- Primary buttons
- Active navigation states
- Key progress indicators
- Important headings or visual anchors

### Accent Pink

Use `#ED64A6` as an accent color. Suggested uses:

- Highlights
- Progress emphasis
- Selected states
- Helpful callouts
- Important buttons or secondary actions when extra emphasis is needed

Use the accent color sparingly so it feels meaningful.

## General UI Tone

The LearnLift AI interface should feel clean, modern, motivational, and focused on progress and confidence. The product should feel professional, friendly, and aspirational. Avoid making the UI look childish.

## Basic Design Principles

- Keep screens focused on one main task.
- Make daily study feel achievable.
- Make study path selection clear and approachable.
- Use clear hierarchy so users can quickly understand what to do next.
- Show progress in a motivating but calm way.
- Prefer simple, readable layouts over decorative complexity.
- Use consistent spacing, typography, and Material 3 components.
- Keep calls to action clear and direct.
- Use brand colors consistently, with purple as the foundation and pink as a controlled accent.
